BTS

A timeline of BTS' major milestones:

  • 2013 - Debuted with their first EP, 2 Cool 4 Skool
  • 2014 - Embarked on their first concert tour, 2014 BTS Live Trilogy Episode II: The Red Bullet
  • 2015 - First music show win with I Need U
  • 2016 - Won their first daesang with their album The Most Beautiful Moment in Life: Young Forever
  • 2017 - Attended their first American award show, winning Top Social Artist at the BBMAs
  • 2018 - Released Love Yourself: Tear, earning their first #1 on the Billboard 200 album chart
  • 2019 - Won Top Duo/Group at the BBMAs
  • 2019 - Embarked on their record-breaking stadium world tour, Speak Yourself
  • 2020 - Released Map of the Soul: 7, earning their 4th Billboard #1 and moving over 400k units
  • 2020 - After the cancellation of their second stadium world tour, Map of the Soul, due to COVID-19, RM confirmed they have began production on a new album
